## Core Story

ArcPay is an operating account for autonomous agent businesses on Somnia.

Show that agents are discoverable services with on-chain identity, pricing, escrowed orders, policy controls, invoices, cards, privacy intents, and audit evidence.

## Flow

1. Start on the landing page and open Docs.
2. Connect the wallet and enter the workspace.
3. Register or load `research-agent`.
4. Show policy controls: spend windows, allowed tokens, allowlist, risk floor, and emergency pause.
5. Open x402 and use `https://x402.20.208.46.195.nip.io`.
6. Quote, pay, verify, fulfill, and unlock a protected agent resource.
7. Show the order state machine.
8. Create a STT or SOMUSD invoice.
9. Show Somnia DeFi adapters on `Swaps` and `Yield`: Somnia Exchange, Somnex, Potion Swap, and custom DEX route intents with required evidence.
10. Create a privacy intent and show release/cancel/disclosure controls.
11. Show SOMUSD cards, contractors, audit, and proofs.

## Closing Line

ArcPay makes Somnia useful for real agent businesses: discover agents, hire them, enforce policy, manage spend, preserve private context, and prove every financial action.
